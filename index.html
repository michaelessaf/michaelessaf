About Me.

I'm an Informatics student at University of Washington who's about to enter the real world. I was born in Colorado and moved 8 times (my parents were military) before I ended up in Seattle. I got my start in tech at a young age when I fell in love with filmmaking. Given my keen interest in visual effects and CGI, I learned a lot about computer hardware and software, and made a bunch of terrible movies along the way. Cut to college and I decided I wanted to dive deeper.

Now, four(!) years later, I specialize in cloud arcitecture, cybersecurity, and backend programming, but I'm handy with front-end and UX stuff too.

Besides coding, I still make films, love art in all of its forms, play guitar (yeah, I'm super unique), stay fit, work on my car, and spend a lot of time thinking about how things look.
